SABR Stress Replay Explorer

SABR final project — interactive demo. Source: github.com/LiangSihan0926/SABR.

This page replays each of the 32 historical SPY single-day drops of −3% or worse (2014–2023) onto a sample portfolio held today (F = 477, T = 30 days). The position is a short 100× 453-strike put. Drag the slider below to swap in different long-put hedges (or no hedge); the per-event PnL bars and the tail-risk readout in the upper-right update live. Built by src/interactive_viz.py.
